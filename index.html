Network design

Software testing is a method of determining whether the actual software product meets the expected requirements and ensuring that the software product is free of defects. It entails running software/system components through their paces using manual or automated tools to evaluate one or more properties of interest. This workshop will provide the participants with a strong base in the field. The workshop will be followed by a hackathon where the participants will put to test the knowledge they've acquired through the workshop.
